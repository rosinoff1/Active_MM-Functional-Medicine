Our Services

We offer personalized, root-cause medicine, from your very first discovery call through long-term transformative care.

Discovery Call

A 15-minute phone call to discuss your health concerns, answer questions about our approach, and determine whether functional medicine is the right fit for you.

Comprehensive Initial Consultation

An in-depth, unrushed first visit where we review your full health history, lifestyle, environment, and goals. This is the foundation of your personalized care plan.

Specialty Lab Testing

Advanced functional lab panels that go beyond conventional bloodwork, including hormone panels, organic acids, stool analysis, food sensitivity testing, and micronutrient assessments interpreted with optimal reference ranges.

Follow-Up Visits

Regular one-on-one appointments to review lab results, adjust protocols, track progress, and provide ongoing support throughout your healing journey.

Personalized Wellness Programs

Structured 3-, 6-, and 9-month programs with frequent visits, specialty testing, unlimited messaging, curated recipes, and supplement support. View our programs →

Virtual Consultations

Telehealth appointments available for Florida and New York residents, bringing the same thorough, personalized care to you from the comfort of your home.

A 15-minute discovery call is the perfect first step. No pressure, no commitment, just a conversation about your health goals.
