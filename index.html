What is Functional Medicine?

Functional medicine is a personalized, science-based approach that empowers patients and practitioners to work together to address the root causes of disease. Instead of treating symptoms in isolation, it looks at the whole picture, your genetics, environment, and lifestyle, to restore balance and lasting health.

Think of it as medicine that asks why, not just what.
