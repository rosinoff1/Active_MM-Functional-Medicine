The Approach

What is Functional Medicine?

Functional medicine identifies and addresses the root cause of disease rather than treating symptoms alone. It views the body as an interconnected system and uses advanced diagnostics to create personalized healing plans.

Conventional Medicine

Focuses on diagnosing diseases and managing symptoms, often through medication. Appointments are typically short, and treatment follows standardized protocols. While effective for acute conditions, it may overlook the underlying drivers of chronic illness.

Functional Medicine

Asks "why" symptoms are occurring by investigating root causes such as gut health, hormone imbalances, toxin exposure, and nutritional deficiencies. Care plans are personalized, thorough, and designed to restore your body's natural ability to heal.
